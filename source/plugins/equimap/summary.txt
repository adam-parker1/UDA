	Count of locus coordinates	EFM_LCFS(N)_(C)

3> Geometrical Axis of boundary (R,Z)	EFM_GEOM_AXIS_R(C), EFM_GEOM_AXIS_Z(C)
4> Minor radius				EFM_MINOR_RADIUS
5> elongation				EFM_ELONGATION
6> Upper triangularity			EFM_TRIANG_UPPER
7> Lower triangularity			EFM_TRIANG_LOWER
8> * magnetic Axis (R,Z)		EFM_MAGNETIC_AXIS_R, EFM_MAGNETIC_AXIS_Z
9> * Bphi at magnetic axis		EFM_BPHI_RMAG

11> * poloidal flux at boundary		efm_psi_boundary
12> * Bphi Vacuum at Reference radius	EFM_BVAC_VAL
13> * Rvac: Reference major radius	EFM_BVAC_R
